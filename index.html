Mind the Gap! About Us

London is filled with spectacular spots for photography with its rich, historic buildings, monuments, famous landmarks as well as breathtaking views. Approximately 30 million tourists visit London per year to experience and capture the country’s vibrant city. Underrated Hotspots London provide you with the best ‘secret’ locations at top London attractions, enabling you to capture unique and creative photographs!

Glass Walkway Tower Bridge

Many people take pictures in front of Tower bridge but not many know that you could climb this! By purchasing tickets at a cheap price unlike other London attractions, you can visit the walkway at the top of tower bridge and see the bridge opening from above. This high level walkway has a mirrored ceiling and a glass floor enabling you to take creative pictures from 42 meters above the River Thames as well as stunning panoramic views of London!

Magic Tunnel King's Cross

Serving as one of the many underground exits from King's Cross Station is a 'Magic Tunnel' with colour changing LED lit walls throughout the 90-meter gently curving tunnel. This spectacular multicoloured light tunnel provides the perfect opportunity to take aesthetic Intagrammable pictures.
